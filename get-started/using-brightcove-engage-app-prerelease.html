Using the Brightcove Engage™ Application for Pre-Release Customers

In this topic you will learn how to install and use the Brightcove Engage application on a mobile device.

Publishing a Mobile App experience will generate an Access Key that is required by the Brightcove Engage application to access the experience on a mobile device.

Installing the iOS native application

The Brightcove Engage mobile app can be installed on iOS devices running iOS 11 or later. Follow these steps to install the app.

  1. Open the following link on your device. 
    https://d1f8qdhgkwmn0p.cloudfront.net/ 
  2. Click the link for the production iOS app.
  3. Click the link to install the Brightcove Engage App on your device.
  4. You will be prompted to confirm the install. Click Install. The app will be installed on your device.
  5. As the app was not installed from the Apple App Store, you need to manually trust the app. Open the device Settings.
  6. Click General > Device Management. Confirm that Brightcove Inc. is listed as an Enterprise App.
  7. Click Brightcove Inc. and then click Trust "Brightcove Inc.".
  8. Click Trust to confirm. The Brightcove Engage app is now ready to use.
  9. Open the Engage app on your device.
  10. Enter the Access Key for the experience.
  11. Click Next.

Depending on how the experience was configured, you may also be prompted to enter your corporate credentials to access the app.

Installing the Android native application

The Brightcove Engage mobile app can be installed on Android devices running Android 5.0 or later. Follow these steps to install the app.

  1. Open the Settings app on your device.
  2. In the Personal section, click Security.
  3. Locate Unknown sources and make sure the Allow installation of apps from sources other than the Play Store is enabled.
  4. Open a browser and go to the following link on your device. 
    https://d1f8qdhgkwmn0p.cloudfront.net/ 
  5. Click the link for the production Android version. This will download the app onto your device.
  6. Click the Open link.
  7. Confirm the install by clicking INSTALL.
  8. When the install has completed, click OPEN to open the app.
  9. Enter the Access Key for the experience.
  10. Click Next.

Depending on how the experience was configured, you may also be prompted to enter your corporate credentials to access the app.

Navigating inside the application

When you open the application, the featured video will appear at the top and the remaining collections will appear below the featured video.

Scroll down to see all the collections and scroll right to see the videos in a collection. Click View All to open a page to view a list of all videos in the collection. Click < at the top of the app to navigate back to the previous page.

Updating the application

Any changes made to an application must be republished inside of the Brightcove Engage application. This may include styling changes or changes to video collections. After the changes are published, users can swipe down on the application to have the application update with the latest published changes.

Searching videos

The search icon () at the top of the app can be used to search for videos. The following video properties will be searched:

  • Name
  • Short description
  • Long description
  • Tags
  • Video ID
  • All custom fields

Changing the Access Key

The Access Key can be changed by clicking the profile icon () at the top of the app. The Profile page will also display the user name and email used to login to the app if SSO is configured.
